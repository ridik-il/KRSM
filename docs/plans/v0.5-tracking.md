## Definition of done (ROADMAP v0.5)

On `kind`, an agent-stamped escaping `delete` is **flagged in audit** and **denied
pre-persistence in enforce**, and an in-scope one is **admitted** — both with a *derived*
scope (no contract) and with a full `TaskContract`. The webhook fails closed on
not-synced/error/timeout/staleness. `closure`/`scope` stay stdlib-only; goldens 01–27
unchanged; `make check` + CI green. The v0.4 gaps #12–#18 are resolved.
